Production notes — content quality standards
Photography
Use high-quality stock of modest Muslim women (Nappy.co, Unsplash, custom shoots). Natural light. Green + gold brand palette. No overly posed shots.
Video style
Reels: vertical 9:16. 3-second hook rule. Captions always on. Trending audio layered under original VO. YouTube: widescreen 16:9.
Carousel design
Consistent brand template. Slide 1 = bold hook. Slide 2–N = content. Last slide = clear CTA. Max 8 slides. Canva or Figma.
Language
English primary. Bengali subtitles on key videos. Arabic phrases used authentically (not performatively). Accessible Islamic vocabulary.

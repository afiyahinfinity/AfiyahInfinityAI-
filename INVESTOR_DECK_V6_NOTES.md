# Afiyah Infinity — UAE & MENA Investor Deck v6

## Positioning
Afiyah Infinity is presented as a women-first technology, education and wellbeing platform combining learning, financial capability, faith-aligned wellbeing and trusted partner access.

## Regulatory boundary
Afiyah does not accept deposits, hold client funds, process payments, provide investment advice, provide medical diagnosis, or certify products as Shariah-compliant. Any future regulated service must be delivered by appropriately licensed partners. KYC, payment, screening and related integrations remain prototype-stage unless separately evidenced.

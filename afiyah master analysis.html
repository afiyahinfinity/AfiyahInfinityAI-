The Afiyah AI Stack — Layer by Layer

Core Intelligence Layer
RAG-Powered Afiyah AI Coach
A Retrieval-Augmented Generation system built on a Shariah-reviewed knowledge base. Users ask questions about Islamic finance, Zakat, halal investing, fasting, wellness — and the Coach retrieves contextually accurate, ADL-vetted responses. Every answer cites its source (Quran, Hadith, AAOIFI standard, or Academy module). No hallucinated fatwas.

Key design principle: the Coach is always in "educational mode", never "advisory mode". If a question crosses into personalised investment advice, it redirects to a licensed partner or a qualified human advisor.
pgvector (Supabase) OpenAI GPT-4o RAG pipeline Citation-first responses ADL content vetting
Personalisation Engine
Intelligent Goal & Wellness Tracker
AI learns each user's financial patterns, faith-cycle events (Ramadan, Hajj saving stage), and life stage (new mother, student, professional). It proactively nudges: "Your Hajj goal needs AED 200 more this month" or "You've achieved Zakat nisab on your savings — here's how to calculate."

Wellness layer: fasting schedule awareness, prayer-time context (no fitness notifications at Fajr), halal nutrition suggestions. Cross-domain AI awareness is the product's key differentiator.
User embeddings Context-aware prompting Supabase realtime Push notifications
Education Intelligence
Adaptive Academy Learning Paths
AI maps each user's knowledge baseline and recommends a personalised learning path. Beginner: "What is Riba?" → Intermediate: "How do Mudarabah returns work?" → Advanced: "How to structure a halal business as Musharakah?"

AI generates practice quiz questions, identifies knowledge gaps, and tracks certification progress. Content is cross-referenced against ADL-approved Shariah sources.
Knowledge graph Adaptive quiz generation Progress ML model AAOIFI curriculum mapping
Community Intelligence
SisTech Smart Matching
AI matches users to the right Sister Circle based on: life stage, financial goals, geographic proximity, language preference (Arabic/Bangla/English), and faith-activity level. Prevents random community formation — makes every connection intentional.

Also matches women entrepreneurs in the Marketplace to relevant mentors, advisors, and potential co-founders from the community.
Embedding similarity Multi-attribute matching Profile vectorization
Smart Finance Tools
AI Zakat Calculator + Sadaqah Optimizer
Beyond simple calculation — AI asks dynamic questions to understand all asset types (gold, savings, business inventory, receivables, investments) and applies the correct AAOIFI Zakat methodology for each. Output: exact Zakat amount + breakdown + suggested distribution partners.

Sadaqah Optimizer: AI recommends giving amounts based on spending patterns, remaining budget, and causes aligned to the user's stated values.
Dynamic form AI AAOIFI Zakat rules engine Charitable partner API
Content Moderation & Safety
Shariah Guardrails System
An AI-powered content filter that prevents the Coach from ever: (a) issuing a fatwa or religious ruling, (b) providing personalised investment advice, (c) recommending specific financial products without licensed partner framing, or (d) contradicting ADL-vetted Shariah positions.

Every AI response is tagged with a confidence score and source reference. Responses below a Shariah-confidence threshold are escalated to human review.
Output filtering Confidence scoring Escalation pipeline ADL review queue
